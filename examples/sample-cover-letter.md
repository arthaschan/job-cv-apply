## 中文版

尊敬的招聘团队：

我写信申请贵公司商务拓展总监一职。拥有12年大客户开发与渠道管理经验，加上AI硕士的技术背景，我能将业务洞察与技术理解结合，推动贵公司在亚太区的战略增长。

在某大型汽车制造企业的12年里，我积累了以下核心能力：

- **大客户开发**：独立开发3家高价值客户，定制"一客一案"解决方案，从0搭建B2B销售管道
- **渠道管理**：管理650人跨区域团队，优化薪酬激励机制，推动一线业务转化效率显著提升
- **扭亏为盈**：接管动荡期亏损门店，13→7人精简实现零纠纷过渡，第3个月扭亏为盈
- **跨区域扩张**：成功复制"某成功案例"至5个城市，奠定全国扩张基础

AI硕士项目（某高校）期间，我开发了基于RoBERTa的双任务分析系统，F1显著提升，验证了AI在业务场景的落地可行性。

期待与您进一步交流。

此致

你的姓名

## English Version

Dear Hiring Team,

I am writing to express my interest in the Business Development Director position at Example Tech Ltd. With 12 years of experience in key account development and channel management, combined with an MSc in Applied AI, I bring a unique blend of business acumen and technical understanding to drive strategic growth across APAC.

Throughout my career at a major automotive manufacturer, I have developed core competencies in:

- **Key Account Development**: Independently secured 3 high-value clients, designing bespoke solutions and establishing a B2B sales pipeline from scratch
- **Channel Management**: Led a 650-person cross-regional team, optimized incentive mechanisms, and significantly improved frontline conversion efficiency
- **Turnaround Leadership**: Took over a loss-making store, streamlined from 13 to 7 staff with zero disputes, achieving profitability by month 3
- **Regional Expansion**: Successfully replicated a proven model to 5 cities, laying the foundation for nationwide scaling

During my MSc in Applied AI at a Hong Kong university, I developed a dual-task analysis system based on RoBERTa, with significantly improved F1, demonstrating the feasibility of AI implementation in business scenarios.

I look forward to discussing how my experience can contribute to Example Tech's goals.

Best regards,

Your Name
