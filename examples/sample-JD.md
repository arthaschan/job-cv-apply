# 示例 JD — Business Development Director

## About the Company

Example Tech Ltd. is a leading technology company specializing in AI-powered enterprise solutions, headquartered in Hong Kong with operations across Asia-Pacific.

## About the Role

We are seeking a dynamic Business Development Director to lead our strategic partnerships and revenue growth initiatives across the APAC region.

## Key Responsibilities

- Develop and execute the APAC business development strategy aligned with company growth targets
- Identify, negotiate, and close strategic partnerships with enterprise clients
- Build and maintain C-suite relationships with key accounts
- Lead a team of 3-5 business development managers
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams to identify market opportunities
- Drive revenue growth through new client acquisition and existing account expansion

## Requirements

- 8+ years of experience in business development, sales, or strategic partnerships
- Proven track record in enterprise B2B sales, preferably in technology/SaaS
- Experience managing and scaling a business development team
- Strong network in the APAC enterprise market
- Excellent negotiation and presentation skills
- Fluent in English; Mandarin preferred
- Bachelor's degree required; MBA preferred

## Nice to Have

- Experience in AI/ML or data analytics solutions
- Background in automotive, manufacturing, or supply chain industries
- Existing relationships with enterprise decision-makers in APAC

## Compensation

- Competitive base salary + performance bonus
- Annual compensation range: HKD 800,000 - 1,200,000
- Equity participation available

## Location

Hong Kong (Hybrid: 3 days in office, 2 days remote)

## 中文翻译

### 关于公司

Example Tech Ltd. 是一家领先的科技公司，专注于AI驱动的企业解决方案，总部位于香港，业务覆盖亚太地区。

### 关于职位

我们正在寻找一位充满活力的商务拓展总监，领导亚太地区的战略合作和收入增长计划。

### 主要职责

- 制定并执行与公司增长目标一致的亚太区商务拓展战略
- 识别、谈判并达成与企业客户的战略合作关系
- 与关键客户建立和维护C-level关系
- 领导3-5名商务拓展经理的团队
- 与产品和工程团队合作，识别市场机会
- 通过新客户获取和现有客户扩展推动收入增长

### 要求

- 8年以上商务拓展、销售或战略合作经验
- 在企业级B2B销售方面有可验证的业绩记录，优先考虑科技/SaaS行业
- 有管理和扩展商务拓展团队的经验
- 在亚太企业市场拥有强大的人脉网络
- 出色的谈判和演示技能
- 流利的英语；优先考虑会普通话者
- 本科学历必须；MBA优先

### 加分项

- 有AI/ML或数据分析解决方案经验
- 有汽车、制造或供应链行业背景
- 在亚太地区与企业决策者有现成关系

### 薪酬

- 有竞争力的底薪 + 绩效奖金
- 年度薪酬范围：HKD 800,000 - 1,200,000
- 可获得股权参与

### 工作地点

香港（混合办公：每周3天办公室，2天远程）
